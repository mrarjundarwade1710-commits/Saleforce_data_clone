How Data Cloud Works

Discover how our real-time customer data engine gathers, maps, and harmonizes enterprise pipelines into actionable unified intelligence.

What is a Customer Data Platform?

A Customer Data Platform (CDP) is packaged software that creates a persistent, unified customer database accessible to other systems. It gathers behavioral, transactional, and demographic data from multiple offline and online touchpoints, resolving identities into a single record.

Unlike legacy database warehouses, Salesforce Data Cloud delivers real-time processing capabilities, letting business systems execute campaigns, trigger support workflows, or update dashboards instantly when new telemetry occurs.

The Data Journey

How Data Cloud connects points across your company step-by-step.

Step 1: Connect & Ingest

Bring data in from web logs, mobile interactions, API streams, CRM records, and external clouds (AWS S3, Azure Blob, Snowflake) using native, zero-copy connectors.

Step 2: Harmonize

Align incoming records against our standard Customer 360 data model. Disparate database tables (like orders, support tickets, web pageviews) are mapped automatically.

Step 3: Unify & Resolve

Run identity resolution rules to deduplicate records. Connect email addresses, browser cookies, and phone numbers into a single, high-fidelity contact card.

Step 4: Analyze & Segment

Build real-time calculated insights (e.g. lifetime value, churn risk score) and construct dynamic segment audiences for immediate activation.

Step 5: Activate & Empower

Stream unified records back into Agentforce AI, marketing execution journeys, Sales dashboards, or advertising ecosystems to personalize every customer touchpoint.

Unifying customer profiles in real time to fuel smarter experiences, workflows, and AI agents across every corporate cloud.
